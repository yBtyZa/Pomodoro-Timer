Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Timer is a time management technique that alternates brief periods of intense work, usually 25 minutes, with regular 5-minute breaks, promoting increased productivity and concentration. During these breaks, it is recommended to perform exercises that help you relax and recharge your energy, such as stretching, deep breathing and brisk walks. These practices help maintain a balance between work and rest, boosting efficiency and well-being throughout the day.
